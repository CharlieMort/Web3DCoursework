About

This was the first model I developed, so was a definite learning experience. I had the idea for recording the timelapses part way through making this model but the total recorded time for this was around 4 hours 15 minutes but of course was longer due to the missing first part.

In the video you see me first draw the textures directly on the wheels, little did i know i wouldnt save that properly and after modeling the handle the previous work would turn black Whoops. This led me to create the textures in Photopea (online photoshop) and learning to figure that out as well. This also taught me alot more about UV unwrapping and the whole process of texturing which i enjoyed.

The animation for this model is quite simple as it was my first time doing animations and in hindsight i could've done this alot easier, I think, but the animations works and is pretty cool - if i had more time i definitely add more effects either in ThreeJS or blender to make the winning spin much more impactful.
