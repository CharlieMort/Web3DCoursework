Claw Machine

Time Taken - 7:34:20

About

I started this model straight after the claw machine but finished it after the robot. It took just under 8 hours in total and was really fun and i learnt the most from this model. I gained the basics after the Claw Machine so went in with a new perspective of what i had to do.

Despite this, the claw defeated me. You can see in the video how long it takes me to model the pincers of the claw - i even switched operating systems instead of coming back to it - curves where almost the end of it but then I mustered the courage and found a different way of doing it that wasnt a perfect curve but looks it from far enough away. I developed the machine by itself and then the claw separately, then scaled down the claw and put it in the machine.

The animation is the most complex of the 3 models, despite being not that complex in my opinion is was quite annoying to do haha. The windows and prize flap also use actual transparent textures to mimic the slight distortion when looking through glass rather than just empty space.
